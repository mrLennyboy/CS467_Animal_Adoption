Introducing Kristoff! Kristof is a handsome 2 year old Pyr mix. He was rescued from a South Texas shelter. He came in very shy and scared. Kristoff is coming out of his shell and learning to trust people. He gets along with other dogs and would benefit from having a confident dog in his new home to help him be the best dog he can be! Kristoff is currently in Texas bit can be on the next NW transport!
