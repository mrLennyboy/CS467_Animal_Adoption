Meet Mollie
You can fill out an adoption application online on our official website.Meet MOLLIE! Mollie is a beautiful 2-3 year old Pyr who weighs about 80 lbs. Mollie was found as a stray and is super sweet. Mollie just wants to be with people and not a working dog. She is great with other dogs of all sizes, good with cats and loves children! Mollie is quickly learning how to be a house dog and could make a great family pet! Mollie is currently in Texas but can be on the next NW transport!
